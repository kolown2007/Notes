Before we explore the concepts we will explain first what is terminal and stations in the common language. In our works we use a lot of borrowed words and giving a new context. In this sense it is also good to explain where we borrow and how did we use it.

In our recent works doesn't view individual show or artwork as the final piece bur rather they are part of the bigger work. In this sense the works now become a system design rather than an object.
But the object in way act as a component in the bigger system design project.

**Stations** - we borrow the concepts of stations from media, weather as well as transport stations. The main characteristics of these are they are part of the node or network. In train stations they don't exist alone but part of the series of stations that made up a line. In the weather stations, they are stationary.
In our work, stations are standing sculptures/ installations. We used stations in the project Colors of the Wind 2 and [[GhostWhispers]].
