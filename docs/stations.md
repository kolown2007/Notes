
**Stations** - we borrow the concepts of stations from media, weather as well as transport stations. The main characteristics of these are they are part of the node or network. In train stations they don't exist alone but part of the series of stations that made up a line. In the weather stations, they are stationary.
In our work, stations are standing sculptures/ installations. We used stations in the project Colors of the Wind 2 and [[GhostWhispers]].
