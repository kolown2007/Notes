**domestic_net** (domestic + internet) is a project **that turns** everyday household objects into internet connection tools.

We propose that this is a natural evolution of internet connection devices—from mainframe computers **(ARPANET)** in the '70s, to PCs in the '90s, laptops in the 2000s, smartphones in the mid-2000s, and smart devices in the 2010s.

While smart devices are common nowadays, we want to turn **existing**, common Filipino household objects—like the feather duster, _walis tingting_, and _walis tambo_—into devices that can perform basic HTTP requests.
