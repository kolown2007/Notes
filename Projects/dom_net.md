**DomNet** (domestic + internet) is a project turning everyday household objects into internet connection tools. We believe that this is a natural evolution of devices—from mainframe computers in the '70s, to PCs in the '90s, laptops in the 2000s, phones in the mid-2000s, and smart devices in the 2010s.

While smart devices are common nowadays, we want to turn already existing, common Filipino household objects—like the feather duster, _walis tingting_, and _walis tambo_—into devices that can perform basic HTTP requests.
